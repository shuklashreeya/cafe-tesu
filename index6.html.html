About Us

Welcome to Café Tesu — a modern café with a focus on premium food tastes. We invite you to celebrate our restaurant's delicious recipes, whether you are here for a leisurely brunch or a relaxed dinner.

Named after the Tesu flower — the flame of the forest — this is the brainchild of entrepreneur Dhruv Goyle, born out of a desire to combine the wonderful worlds of food, coffee, art, and imagination.

Located at the entrance of Essex Farms on Sri Aurobindo Marg, our striking blue-façade café with over 50 covers has become South Delhi's favourite gathering place. Open every day from 8 AM to midnight.
